Organize yourselves; prepare every needful thing; and establish a house, even a house of prayer, a house of fasting, a house of learning, a house of glory, a house of order, a house of God.
Doctrine and Covenants 88:118

A temple is literally the house of God on earth. As part of the dispensation of the fulness of times, God has once again restored many essential ordinances and covenants back to the earth. In temples, we are able to partake of those ordinances, making covenants with God and receiving blessings in return. One of the most important ordinances is the sealing of families for time and all eternity. This ordinance takes place in the holy temples of God. This allows families to be together forever; not just until death!
